Profile

Research

I work on the mission architectures and starlight-suppression instruments behind direct imaging of potentially habitable worlds — self-consistent starshade concepts (occulter geometry, inner working angle, formation-flying separation) and yield modeling with EXOSIMS/AYO for NASA's Habitable Worlds Observatory. Co-author on HWO and starshade studies indexed in NASA ADS; active in HWO working groups and SGAC.

Engineering

I build the systems that get missions and their data working: satellite architecture modeling, command & data handling, and SPENVIS radiation-dose analysis, plus production Python/SQL pipelines, ML models, and workflow automation delivered across consulting, enterprise, and startup settings — including co-founding and leading engineering for a multilingual voice-agent platform.
